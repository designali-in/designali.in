# The Ultimate Guide to LinkedIn Post Dimensions: Essential Insights

LinkedIn is a powerful platform for professionals to network, share insights,
and promote their brands. To make the most of your LinkedIn presence, it's
crucial to understand the ideal dimensions for various post types. Here's a
comprehensive guide to help you optimize your LinkedIn content.

## Profile Picture

<SmallImage
  src="/images/guides/images/linkedin-logo.jpg"
  width={200}
  height={200}
  alt=""
/>

- Recommended Size: 400 x 400 pixels
- File Type: PNG or JPEG
- Tip: Choose a high-quality, professional photo that clearly represents you or
  your brand.

## Cover Photo

<Image
  src="/images/guides/images/linkedin-cover.jpg"
  width={1800}
  height={200}
  alt=""
/>

- Recommended Size: 1584 x 396 pixels
- File Type: PNG or JPEG
- Tip: Use this space to convey your brand's message or showcase a visually
  appealing image related to your field.

## Company Logo

- Recommended Size: 300 x 300 pixels
- File Type: PNG or JPEG
- Tip: Ensure your logo is clear and recognizable at this size, as it represents
  your company across the platform.

## Banner Image for Company Page

- Recommended Size: 1128 x 191 pixels
- File Type: PNG or JPEG
- Tip: Utilize this banner to highlight your company's achievements or latest
  updates.

## Shared Image

- Recommended Size: 1200 x 627 pixels
- File Type: PNG or JPEG
- Tip: Choose eye-catching visuals that will engage your audience and encourage
  interaction.

## Shared Video

- Recommended Size: Minimum width of 256 pixels and a maximum of 4096 x 2304
  pixels
- Length: 3 seconds to 10 minutes
- File Type: MP4
- Tip: Keep videos concise and engaging to capture your audience's attention
  quickly.

## Article Images

- Recommended Size: 744 x 400 pixels
- File Type: PNG or JPEG
- Tip: Use relevant and high-quality images to complement your article and
  attract readers.

## Ad Images

- Recommended Size: 1200 x 627 pixels
- File Type: PNG or JPEG
- Tip: Craft visually appealing ads that align with your campaign goals and
  target audience.

## Carousel Images

- Recommended Size: 1080 x 1080 pixels
- File Type: PNG or JPEG
- Tip: Utilize multiple images to tell a story or convey a more detailed
  message.

### General Tips

- File Size: Keep images under 8 MB for best performance.
- Aspect Ratio: Maintain recommended aspect ratios to prevent distortion or
  cropping.
- Consistency: Use a consistent style and branding across all your visuals for a
  cohesive look.

By adhering to these guidelines, you can ensure that your LinkedIn content is
visually appealing and optimized for engagement. Make the most of your LinkedIn
presence by leveraging the right dimensions for each type of post.
